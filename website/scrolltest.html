  • RETREAD(DEBUGGING):

    Do you have an eye for errors?
    This is your place!

    Description:

    Round 1:
    30 questions, of general debugging (or) output estimations in C/C++/Java
    Teams with most number of correctly answered questions qualify. Starred questions are considered in case of a tie.

    Round 2:
    Shortlisted contestants will be asked to construct programs for the given problem specification in C/C++/java.
    The results are based on number of correctly solved problems and also efficiency of the code. Time and/or space constraints may be specified.

    Rules:
    1) Each team can comprise of not more than 2 members (lone wolves are also entertained).
    2) No contestant can be a member of more than one team.
    3) The participants needn’t be from the same college/institution.
    4) Contestants are not allowed to carry any additional materials.
    5) Teams involving in any kind of malpractice will be immediately disqualified.
    6) Decisions made by the judges will be final.
    7) The participants are not allowed to use any Compiler/IDE in the 1st round(debugging round).

  • ENTRE VISTA(MOCK INTERVIEW):

    DESCRIPTION:



    A mock interview is an emulation of an interview used for training purposes. The interview tries to resemble a real interview as close as possible. This event is similar to attending an interview for an IT Company. In this you have all the similar levels like aptitude where you will have quantitative aptitude round and a technical round. Then the students who are eligible will be attending an interview which will again have a Technical and a HR round. Many of the questions that will be asked are interview questions. Based on the session the feedback on the interview process and the marks will be awarded. The Mock Interview focuses on how well you know yourself and your past experiences, how well you know the industry you hope to enter, and how well you can convey that information. You will gain the most experience from your Mock Interview, if you treat it like an actual interview. The Mock Interview is a safe place to practice your interview skills and gain feedback.

    ROUND 1:

    Non Technical Quiz

    ROUND 2:

    Technical Quiz

    ROUND 3:

    Interview (Technical and HR)

    Rules:
    1) Students must compulsorily bring their resume.
    2) Students are requested to come in formals.
    3) Judges decision is final.

  • 3-Dimension


    "Its an event that checks your thinking,memory and creativity".

    • There are three rounds in this event, namely
      • Pictionary.
      • Listen and Answer.
      • Adzap.
        •

      Description:-

      First round:Pictionary

      • Any one person from a team will be given a word and he/she must use drawings to express the given word to his/her team.
        •

      Second round:Listen And Answer

      • An audio will be played to the team and the team members should guess the voice of the person in the audio.

      Third round: Adzap

      • A product will be given to the team and the team should act on how will they creatively market their product.
      • 2 mins preparation time will be given and acting time will be between 1-4 min.
